Hi! We're TeamVulpine.

We're a team of game developers, aiming to make game engine programming more accessible. We're accomplishing this by developing a new game engine, called PawprintEngine, and open sourcing core systems and components.

In addition to the engine, we're also developing a couple games, some use Pawprint, and others don't. Our main project, and the test case for Pawprint, is Stratalia, a voxel based sandbox.

Stay tuned, there's more to come!
